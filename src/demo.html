Waveform Audio Player Demo

A demo of the Waveform Audio Player.

Player with JavaScript

This is how the player looks in modern browsers with JavaScript enabled (assuming, of course, that you're currently using such a browser):

  • Annoying drunk guy howling

    You know that guy who is having lots of fun outside at 3am and wants the whole street to know about it? Yeah, that guy.

    0:00 - Eeeeeeeou!

    0:02 - Oow!

    0:04.8 - Errrrrrrr!

    0:08 - Oow!

    0:10 - Yehay!

    0:11 - Yeeeee... Oow!

    0:14 - Woooooo!

  • Beatbox (presented without comment)
  • Baby
    A baby moaning. I assume that the baby is saying something along the lines of “WTF dad, why have you taken me into this odd room with egg boxes on the walls and surrounded me with microphones?”
  • Synth chords

    Two repetitions of a synth pad.

    0:00 - First repetition

    0:05 - Second repetition.

  • London Underground, Arriving

    Raw audio of a London Underground train arriving on the Bakerloo line at London Waterloo. The classic "Mind the Gap" can also be heard. Taken around 1:15PM on the weekend, so very crowded (though, you'd be hard pressed to find a time when it isn't crowded!)

    0:00 - Bustle on a station platform.

    0:07 - Train starts to arrive

    0:16 - Wheel screech

    0:28 - Mind the gap please!

    0:34 - Mind the gap please (2)!

    0:39 - Mind the gap please (3)!

  • Cinematic Strings
    Made with vst instruments.
